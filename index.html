Paul Gauguin, a famous French post-Impressionist artist, quitted his job as a stockbroker, left his family and became an artist in Tahiti where he created a few of the most world-renowned artworks. Inspired by his life story, line ESCAPE explores the tension between external constraints and personal realizations. Our line utters a conflicting emotion emerging from the collision between the ideal - one’s pursuit of freedom and the reality - one’s responsibility for family, social expectation, etc. We wish to encourage people to eventually break restraints and realize aspirations.
