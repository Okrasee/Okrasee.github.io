Hi, I'm Yirui Zhu.

Nice to meet you :)

I was born and raised in Shanghai. I studied Math at Carnegie Mellon and moved to New York after graduation in 2020. I currently work as a data engineer at MediaMath. I was always looking for connections between Math and Art, two of my favorite disciplines, in high school. And there began my journey of interdisciplinary explorations! I am dedicated to connect math, data, science, design, art and other fields together to solve problems.

I want to leverage my technical knowledge and skills to create impacts on a larger scope - solve critical social issues! Thus I have continuously particpated in ML/AI-driven volunteering activites. I am also applying for a chapter lead position at Omdena to oversee projects in the US. Will I get it? Stay tuned.

In my spare time, I create art. Oil paint, watercolor, sketch, digital, crayon...I try different mediums.
